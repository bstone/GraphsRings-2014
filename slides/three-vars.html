The Three Variable Case

Proposition:

Let $S$ be $\Bbbk[x_1,x_2,x_3]$, and let $I = (f_1,f_2,f_3)$ be an ideal generated by a homogeneous regular sequence with $\deg(f_i) = e_i$ where $e_i \leq e_{i+1}$ for all $i$. Then

\begin{align*} \beta(S/I) = &\,\,\,\,\,\ e_1e_2(e_2+e_3)\cdot\pi(0,e_1,e_1+e_2,e_1+e_2+e_3)\\ & + e_1e_2(e_3-e_1)\cdot \pi(0,e_2,e_1+e_2,e_1+e_2+e_3)\\ & + 2e_1e_2(e_1+e_3-e_2)\cdot \pi(0,e_2,e_1+e_3,e_1+e_2+e_3)\\ & + e_1e_2(e_3-e_1)\cdot \pi(0,e_3,e_1+e_3,e_1+e_2+e_3)\\ & + e_1e_2(e_2+e_3)\cdot \pi(0,e_3,e_2+e_3,e_1+e_2+e_3). \end{align*}
