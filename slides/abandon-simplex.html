Abandoning the simplex for the cone

The cone of Betti diagrams has a natural multiplication operation induced by the tensor product operation on complexes.

Definition

Let $\beta$ and $\beta'$ be Betti diagrams. The tensor product of $\beta$ and $\beta'$ is the diagram $\beta \cdot \beta'$ defined by \[ (\beta \cdot \beta')_{i,j} = \sum_{\substack{i_1+i_2=i\\j_1+j_2=j}}\beta_{i_1,j_1}\cdot\beta_{i_2,j_2}'. \]
